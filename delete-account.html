Delete Your GymZy Account

We're sorry to see you go! This page explains how to request deletion of your GymZy account and associated data.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your workout data, exercise history, and progress will be permanently deleted.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from within the GymZy app (Recommended)
  1. Open the GymZy app
  2. Go to Profile tab at the bottom
  3. Scroll down to the "Danger Zone" section
  4. Tap "Delete Account & Data"
  5. Confirm your deletion request

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately.

Option 2: Email us directly

Send an email to teamgymzy@gmail.com with:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • The email address associated with your GymZy account
  • Your name (as it appears in the app)

We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Profile Information: Name, email address, and profile picture
  • Workout Data: All your workout history, exercises, sets, reps, and weights
  • Body Measurements: All body measurement records you've entered
  • Progress Photos: Any photos you've uploaded (if applicable)
  • App Preferences: Settings and customizations
  • Analytics Data: Usage patterns and app interaction data

Data Retention Period

Upon receiving your deletion request:

  • Immediate: Your account will be deactivated and you will no longer be able to sign in
  • Within 30 days: All your personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers
  • Up to 90 days: Backup copies may be retained for disaster recovery purposes, then permanently deleted

What Data May Be Retained

We may retain the following anonymized data for legal and operational purposes:

  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data (no personal identifiers)
  • Transaction records if required by law (payment history, if applicable)
  • Data necessary to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes
