Privacy Policy for GymZy

Last Updated: December 8, 2025

Introduction

GymZy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use GymZy, we may collect:

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture (if available)
  • Authentication Data: User ID and authentication tokens provided by Google Sign-In
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for analytics purposes

Workout Data

We collect and store:

  • Workout history (exercises, sets, reps, weights, duration, distance)
  • Workout templates you create
  • Body measurements you choose to track (weight, body fat percentage, etc.)
  • Exercise preferences and custom notes

Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect:

  • App usage statistics
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Session duration and frequency

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Store and sync your workout data across devices
  • Personalize Experience: Remember your preferences and workout history
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Ensure Security: Protect your account and prevent unauthorized access

Data Storage and Security

Cloud Storage

  • Your workout data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore
  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Data at rest is encrypted by Firebase's security infrastructure
  • We follow industry-standard security practices

Local Storage

  • Theme preferences and cached data are stored locally on your device
  • This data does not leave your device

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your workout data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes without consent

We MAY share data only:

  • With service providers (Google Firebase, Google Analytics) to operate the app
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • In the event of a merger or acquisition (with prior notice)

Third-Party Services

GymZy uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: Provides authentication services - Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Google): Cloud Firestore, Authentication, Analytics - Firebase Privacy Policy

Your Data Rights

Access Your Data

  • View all your workout data within the app
  • Export your data by contacting us

Delete Your Data

You can delete your data by:

  • Using the "Delete My Data" option in the app's Profile settings
  • This will permanently delete all your workouts, templates, measurements, and account data
  • Alternatively, email us at teamgymzy@gmail.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"

Modify Your Data

  • You can edit or delete workouts, templates, and measurements directly in the app

Children's Privacy

GymZy is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

  • Active Users: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted immediately upon using the in-app deletion feature
  • Inactive Accounts: We may delete accounts inactive for more than 2 years after notification

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app and on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification (for major changes)

Your continued use of GymZy after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Google Play Store requirements
  • Apple App Store requirements
